## NVKVS: The implementation of WiscKey with Pmem in RocksDB

The modification was written by Ryan Nathanael Soenjoto Widodo (me) when I was a PhD. student in OSSS Laboratory in the University of Tsukuba under the guidance of Professor Kazuhiko Kato and Professor Hirotake Abe.

For building, please follow the building guide of RocksDB with some modification of libpmem.

Paper:
https://www.jstage.jst.go.jp/article/ipsjjip/30/0/30_332/_pdf
